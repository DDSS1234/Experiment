Material Study

Compressed Cellulose Chair 05

2025

Medium Compressed cellulose, mineral pigment, linen restraint
Dimensions 92 × 68 × 71 cm
Status Prototype, archive held
Series Cellulose Pressure Studies
Location ROS-NO Materials Lab, North Wing

The fifth chair compresses cellulose into a dense, breathable structure that remembers the pressure of its mold. Pigment is mixed during curing to retain a muted warmth, allowing the object to shift tone across daylight. The piece is held in the ROS-NO archive as a study in tension and softness, balancing mass with the pull of linen restraint.
